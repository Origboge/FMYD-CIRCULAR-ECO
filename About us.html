ABOUT US.

The Waste to Wealth Initiative is a groundbreaking project designed specifically to create wealth / job opportunities for youths by transform waste into Valuable resources & Eco-friendly items, promoting a circular economy and sustainable waste management practices across the 36 states of Nigeria and FCT. Implemented by the Federal Ministry of Youth Development on the mandate of president Bola Ahmed Tinubu, this initiative will empower young Nigerians to become job creators, develop waste-to-wealth businesses, contributing to achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), mitigating climate change effect, reducing environmental pollution and fostering economic growth. The project aims to train youth on how to make money from Plastic Recycling, Paper Recycling, Metal Recycling & Scrabs, Glass Recycling, E Waste Recycling, Textiles Recycling, Waste Valorization to Animal Feeds & Fertilizers, Energy & Community Renewable Solutions, wind turbines etc.
